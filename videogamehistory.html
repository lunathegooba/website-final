The history of Video Games!

1952

Video games started in a research lab full of scientists.In 1952, a British professor, A.S. Douglas created “OXO” (A version of tic tac toe) as part of his doctoral dissertation at the university of cambridge.

1958

In 1958, Tennis for Two (also known as the first ever video game made), was created by an American physicist William Higinbotham in 1958. It was made on a large analog computer and connected to an oscilloscope screen for the annual visitor’s day at the Brookhaven National Laboratory in Uptown, New York.

1962

In 1962, Steve Russell at the Massachusetts institute of technology invented “Space war!” a computer based-combat video game for the PDP-1. This was the first video game that could be played on multiple computer installations.
